Instructions
  1. Light: Keep in low to medium indirect light.
  2. Water: Water when the top inch of soil is dry. Avoid overwatering.
  3. Temperature: Ideal range is 60-75°F (15-24°C).
  4. Humidity: Average household humidity is fine.
  5. Soil: Use well-draining potting mix.
  6. Fertilizer: Feed with diluted liquid fertilizer every 2-4 weeks in spring and summer.
